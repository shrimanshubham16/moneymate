# FinFlow Features

Complete overview of all features in FinFlow.

## 🎯 Core Features

### Financial Health Score
- **Real-time calculation** of your month-end financial position
- **Visual indicators** (green/yellow/red) based on configurable thresholds
- **Breakdown view** showing income, expenses, investments, and dues
- **Consistent across** dashboard, health page, and exports

### Income Management
- **Multiple income sources**: Salary, freelance, rental, RSUs
- **Flexible frequencies**: Monthly, quarterly, yearly
- **RSU Support**: 
  - Grant count and vesting schedule
  - Live stock price fetching
  - Currency conversion
  - Tax withholding calculation
  - Expected decline buffer
- **Include/Exclude toggle**: Choose which incomes affect health calculation

### Expense Tracking

#### Fixed Expenses
- **Recurring monthly costs**: Rent, subscriptions, EMIs
- **SIP flag**: Mark investments (mutual funds, PPF, etc.)
- **Category tagging**: Organize by type
- **Payment tracking**: Mark as paid/unpaid each month

#### Variable Expenses
- **Budget limits**: Set monthly spending caps
- **Actual tracking**: Record real spending
- **Overspend detection**: Automatic warnings when over budget
- **Constraint score**: Gradual cooldown for overspending habits

### Investment Management
- **SIP tracking**: Monthly investment commitments
- **Accumulated funds**: Track total invested amount
- **Priority/Critical flag**: Protect important investments
- **Pause/Resume**: Temporarily exclude from calculations
- **Withdrawal support**: Use accumulated funds for bomb defusal

### Credit Cards & Loans
- **Multiple cards**: Track spending across cards
- **Due dates**: Never miss a payment
- **Usage tracking**: Monitor spending vs. limit
- **Loan management**: Track EMIs and repayment

### Future Bombs
- **Large expense planning**: Plan for upcoming big costs
- **Smart defusal strategies**:
  1. Pause non-critical investments
  2. Sell RSU stocks (with tax/decline calculations)
  3. Withdraw from investment accumulated funds
  4. Custom mix of all strategies
- **Real-time feedback**: See how each strategy affects your SIP requirement
- **Activity logging**: Track all bomb-related actions

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

### End-to-End Encryption
- **Client-side encryption**: Your data is encrypted before sending
- **Server can't read**: Encrypted fields are unreadable by server
- **Numeric fields plaintext**: For server-side calculations only

### Account Security
- **Strong passwords**: Enforced requirements
- **Account lockout**: After 3 failed attempts
- **Recovery keys**: Password reset via saved key
- **No email dependency**: Privacy-first approach

### Data Isolation
- **User-specific data**: Complete isolation between users
- **Shared view security**: Partners can only view, not modify
- **RLS policies**: Database-level access control
